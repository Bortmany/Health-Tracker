Cut design system
Foundations — color, type, spacing

Cut keeps its dark + lime identity and matures it: one brand accent, a real set of feedback and chart roles, and proper scales for type and spacing. Feedback colors are never used to shame a missed target.

The lime is for actions, active states and the primary chart line — brand, not wallpaper. Nobody else in the category owns this color; the incumbents are blue (MyFitnessPal, Trainerize) or coral (Noom).

#6ee787

Danger marks destructive actions and errors only — never an over-target calorie count or a missed habit. That rule comes straight from what users love about MacroFactor and hate about the shaming apps.

Every weight chart shows two lines: the pale raw scale readings underneath, the smooth lime trend on top. Users see direction, not daily noise.
