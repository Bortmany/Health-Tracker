## Product
- **What it is (one paragraph):** A home coffee business in Oman ("Pouring love into every cup") that runs on Instagram and WhatsApp — now with its own ordering web store (the standalone Bean & Brew app). Customers browse the real menu in English/Arabic, pin their delivery location on a map, and send the order; it lands in the owner's live dashboard and opens WhatsApp with the order pre-written. Server-verified stamp-card loyalty (8 stamps = a free drink of choice) and distance-based delivery zones with free delivery above 10 OMR.
