# Brand profile: Oman Property Intelligence

## Product
- **What it is (one paragraph):** A bilingual (English/Arabic, full RTL) real-estate data and analysis platform for Oman — market dashboard, neighborhood pages, area comparison, and calculators (rental yield, mortgage with conventional and Islamic modes, full ROI), all in OMR. **The honest-data promise IS the brand:** every figure on the site carries a label saying where it came from (Verified, Official statistic, User submitted, AI estimated) plus a confidence score — estimates are never dressed up as facts.
