# Ad-account registry

Every ad account the owner opens gets one row here, filled in by the owner (or by Claude at the owner's dictation). **A row in this table with an owner-set monthly cap is the evidence the activation switchboard checks** for waking `ads-manager` and `ad-creative-producer` — no row, no ads work, full stop. Agents never touch the accounts themselves; this table just records that they exist and what the owner allows.
